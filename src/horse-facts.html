Interesting Facts About Horses

  • The first cloned horse was a Haflinger mare in Italy in 2003.
  • When horses look like they're laughing, they're actually engaging in a special nose-enhancing technique known as "flehmen".
  • You can tell if a horse a mare or a stallion by the amount of teeth they have.
  • The average horse's heart weighs about 9 or 10 pounds.
  • Horses have around 205 bones in their skeleton, while humans have 206!
